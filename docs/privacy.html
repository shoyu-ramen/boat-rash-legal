Boat Rash Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 13, 2026 · Contact: boat-rash-privacy@rosskuehl.com

Boat Rash is a single-player and local multiplayer arcade boat racing game. It is designed to work fully offline.

1. Information we collect

Boat Rash does not collect personal information. There are no accounts, analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, crash reporters that transmit data off-device, or third-party tracking.

The app stores the following data only on your device:

  • Career progress (cash, unlocks, garage loadout, race history)
  • Settings preferences (audio, HUD, assist options)
  • Optional local ghost lap recordings and personal bests

2. How data is used

Local save data exists only so the game can restore your progress and preferences. It is never uploaded to our servers, because the game has no backend network services.

3. Network access

Boat Rash does not require an internet connection to play. It does not send gameplay or personal data over the network. System-level features outside the app (for example, installing the app from the App Store or TestFlight) are governed by Apple’s policies.

4. Children

Boat Rash does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app does not collect personal data, it is suitable for use under parental guidance according to the App Store age rating.

5. Third parties

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties. There are no advertising partners or analytics vendors embedded in the app.

6. Data retention & deletion

All save data remains on your device until you delete the app or use any in-app reset option (if provided). Uninstalling Boat Rash removes local app data subject to your device’s normal storage behavior.

7. Changes

If this policy changes in a material way, we will update the effective date on this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

Questions about privacy: boat-rash-privacy@rosskuehl.com
